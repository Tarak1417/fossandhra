Welcome to Adarsh English Medium School

Adarsh English Medium School is proud to be recognized as the best English medium educational institution in Tenali, serving the community with excellence for over 35 years. Our student-centric approach, combined with a strong focus on the overall development of each child, sets us apart as a trusted choice for families seeking quality education at an affordable cost. 

We believe in nurturing young minds with care, knowledge, and values, making Adarsh a leading destination for parents who want the best for their children’s academic and personal growth.

Our Mission

To provide a holistic and accessible English medium education that fosters academic excellence, ethical integrity, and personal growth, ensuring that every student is equipped to navigate life’s challenges and lead with compassion and confidence.

Our Vision

To be a leading English medium educational institution that empowers every child with the wisdom, knowledge, and values needed to succeed in life and contribute positively to society, while ensuring education is accessible to all.

Our Motto

At Adarsh English Medium School, our guiding principle is encapsulated in our motto: ‘Wisdom is Light.’ This belief drives everything
we do, inspiring us to enlighten minds and shape futures with the power of knowledge and strong values.

A Holistic Approach to Education

At Adarsh, we understand that true education goes beyond textbooks. We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and cultural events, to ensure that every child has the opportunity to explore their interests and develop their talents.

Growth and Expansion

From our humble beginnings, Adarsh has grown into a respected institution with six campuses across Tenali and its surrounding areas. We now serve over 2,600 students, each of whom is supported by our team of more than 100 dedicated teachers and staff.

Why Choose Adarsh?

Academic Excellence

At Adarsh English Medium School, we prioritize academic excellence, offering a rigorous curriculum that challenges and inspires students. Our experienced faculty guides each student toward achieving their full potential, ensuring a strong foundation in core subjects and preparing them for success in higher education and beyond.

Holistic Development

We believe in nurturing the whole child at Adarsh. Our holistic approach includes not only academic learning but also physical education, arts, and moral development. By fostering creativity, teamwork, and ethical values, we help students become well-rounded individuals ready to thrive in all aspects of life.

Innovative Learning

Adarsh embraces innovative learning techniques that make education engaging and effective. With the integration of technology in our classrooms, we encourage interactive and experiential learning. Our approach ensures that students develop critical thinking skills and adapt to the ever-changing demands of the modern world.

Ethical Foundation

At Adarsh, education is rooted in strong ethical values. We emphasize the importance of honesty, integrity, and respect, guiding students to become compassionate and responsible citizens. Our ethical foundation shapes not just their academic journey, but also their character, preparing them to lead with wisdom and kindness.

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Our state-of-the-art infrastructure provides a safe, comfortable, and inspiring environment for learning. With smart classrooms, well-equipped labs, and modern amenities, we offer students the best facilities to support their academic and extracurricular pursuits, ensuring they have everything they need to succeed.

Accessible Education for All

We are committed to providing accessible education to all children, regardless of their background. Adarsh offers quality education at an affordable cost, ensuring that every child has the opportunity to benefit from our comprehensive programs. We believe in equal opportunities for every student to learn and grow.
